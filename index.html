How to identify and confirm the quality of fish

  • A shiny skin with slime on the surface is a good sign that the fish is fresh and good quality.
  • The fins should be clearly defined and perky, not scraggy or broken.
  • A moist, shiny and pink or red gill is a great sign that the fish is fresh and good quality. Stay away from fish that has brown or faded gills.
  • Remember fresh fish should smell of the sea not fish. Also make sure that the fish smells appetising and not of a smelly odour that you hope will disappear when you cook it.
